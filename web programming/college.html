About Us

Government Engineering College Thrissur (GEC Thrissur), formerly known as Government Engineering College Trichur, is an ISO 9001:2008-certified institution and holds the prestigious position of being ranked second among engineering colleges in Kerala in both NIRF 2024 and KIRF 2024 rankings. Established in 1957, the college is one of South Indiaâ€™s most reputed technical education institutions. The foundation stone of the college's main building was laid by Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru, the then Prime Minister of India. Located on a sprawling 75-acre campus at Ramavarmapuram, just 5 km from Thrissur city, the college features state-of-the-art infrastructure and proximity to key transport hubs, including the Thrissur railway station and Cochin Airport.

Over the years, GEC Thrissur has grown into one of the most sought-after institutions for engineering education, offering eight full-time undergraduate programs, B.Arch, , twenty postgraduate programs, and Ph.D. research opportunities. Additionally, the college provides postgraduate programs in MPlan and an MCA program. Affiliated with APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University, the college is recognized by AICTE and COA, with its programs accredited by NBA. The campus boasts extensive academic and administrative facilities, hostels, a library, a central computing facility, sports amenities, and recreational spaces. GEC Thrissur is renowned for its excellent placement record, consistently achieving near 100% placements. With a legacy of distinguished alumni excelling as executives, entrepreneurs, and leaders globally, the institution remains a cornerstone of engineering education in Kerala.
